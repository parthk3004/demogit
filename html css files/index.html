Different services provided by us

3D Animation

At Born Artist, our skilled animators begin the 3D animation process by using polygons to create figures. Digital armatures are then used to bring the figures to life. We can breathe life into 3D characters and images for your movie, game, product demos, website, television program or presentation.

2D Animation


We create 2D characters or images by using computer adaptations of traditional animation techniques. Bitmap graphics or 2D vector graphics are first created and then animated.

MOTION GRAPHICS

We turn your idea into a short animation using engaging motion graphics which keep your audience interested in the story.
we create visually stunning pieces for a broad range of applications including TV commercials, promotional web videos, etc..

GRAPHIC DESIGN

Depending on the scope of your project
we can create something as simple as a basic caption all the way to fully animated
elements, elevating the visual impact of your content.
